Biography

Hailing from Austin, TX and playing their own unique style dubbed as "Electro Tribe", Trouble in The Streets has moved up quickly in the local and vibrant musical culture surrounding them. Mixing a blend of influences from electronic, to world, hip-hop, rock, and r&b, Trouble in The Streets continues to leave a big musical impression everywhere they play. Most recently they have shared stages with George Clinton and the Parliament Funkadelic, 2012 Grammy Award Winners Rebirth Brass Band, and Beto Martinez of the 2011 Grammy Award winning project Grupo Fantasma. In 2016, TiTS band was a runner up for Best New Austin Band in the Austin Music Awards. Trouble in The Streets offers an eclectic experience that breaks the fourth wall and invites audiences to create their own story.
